About Us

From Our Hearts, To Your Doorstep.

Welcome to River Dachs Miniature Dachshunds, where our love for these charming little dogs is at the heart of everything we do. We are passionate about thoughtfully pairing and raising puppies with exceptional temperament, structure, and health.

Each puppy is raised right here in our home—not in a kennel—surrounded by the daily rhythm of a busy household with other dogs, curious cats, and plenty of family activity. They are gently socialized with as many people as possible, including children, so they grow into confident, well-adjusted companions who are ready to handle the world with ease.

From their very first days, we invest time, love, and attention into shaping balanced, affectionate puppies who are prepared to thrive in their forever homes. We are honored to be part of your journey in finding the perfect miniature dachshund, and we can't wait to introduce you to your next best friend.
